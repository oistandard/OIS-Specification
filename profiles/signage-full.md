# Profile: Signage Provider (Full Stack)

## Required layers

* OIS-Core
* OIS-Display
* OIS-Media
* OIS-Proof-of-Play

## Optional layers

* OIS-Events
* OIS-Location
* OIS-Commerce
* OIS-Sense

## Primary endpoints

* `GET /.well-known/ois`
* `POST /display/screens`
* `POST /media/deliveries`
* `POST /proof-of-play/events`
